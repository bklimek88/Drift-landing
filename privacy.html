Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 11, 2026

1. Overview

Drift Labs ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal information.

2. Data We Collect

We collect the following categories of data:

  • Account data: email address, name, payment information (processed by Paddle)
  • Usage data: feature usage statistics, error logs, session duration
  • Voice data: voice commands are processed locally on your device and via third-party AI APIs (Anthropic Claude, Deepgram). We do not store voice recordings.
  • Device data: operating system version, hardware identifiers for license validation

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and maintain the Service
  • To process payments and manage subscriptions
  • To send important product updates and security notices
  • To improve the Service through aggregated, anonymized analytics
  • To validate software licenses

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with:

  • Paddle — payment processing and subscription management
  • Anthropic — AI language model processing (voice commands)
  • Deepgram — speech-to-text processing
  • ElevenLabs — text-to-speech processing

Each third party is bound by their own privacy policies and data processing agreements.

5. Data Storage and Security

Drift runs primarily on your local Windows device. We apply industry-standard security measures to protect any data transmitted to our servers. Payment data is never stored by us — it is handled entirely by Paddle.

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to processing of your data
  • Data portability

To exercise these rights, contact us at hello@usedrift.ai

7. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your subscription is active, plus 90 days after cancellation. You may request immediate deletion by contacting us.

8. Children's Privacy

Drift is not intended for users under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email. Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance.

10. Contact

Questions about privacy? Email us at hello@usedrift.ai
